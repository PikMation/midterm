Introduction

Welcome to my Portfolio website where you'll see all my work made in display from the past. I set up this website to show you my works that I created to demostrate my works and skills that I have. My name is Cesar Casillas Orozco, I am a deaf person working to become a Game Designer/Animator. I overcomed many obstacles and created many artworks, models, animations, and game I made! If you wish to learn more about me, you can click the About Me tabs to see more information.

Skills

The skills that I have have been varied that I learned from my high school years and college years. The skills I learn is digital art, 2D arts animation, 3D modeling and animation, and game design.
